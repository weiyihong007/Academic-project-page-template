Monet: General Image Generation Agent via MCTS and online-RL

Weiyi Hong
Xi'an Jiaotong University
Paper Code arXiv

Monet unifies Monte-Carlo Tree Search and online reinforcement-learning to generate high-quality images from arbitrary prompts.

Abstract

We present Monet, a general-purpose image-generation agent that combines Monte-Carlo Tree Search (MCTS) with online reinforcement-learning (online-RL) to achieve state-of-the-art fidelity and diversity. By treating the generation process as a sequential decision-making problem, Monet uses MCTS to explore promising paths in the latent space and continuously refines its policy through online-RL. Extensive experiments demonstrate superior performance across multiple benchmarks and prompt regimes.
